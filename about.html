About Gyanith 25

Gyanith 25 is NIT Puducherry's flagship technical festival, bringing together the brightest minds to explore the future of human-machine collaboration.

Innovation

Fostering groundbreaking ideas at the intersection of human creativity and artificial intelligence.

Technology

Showcasing cutting-edge technological advancements and their impact on our future.

Collaboration

Building bridges between human expertise and machine capabilities for enhanced problem-solving.

Global Impact

Creating solutions that address real-world challenges and benefit society at large.

National Institute of Technology Puducherry

NIT Puducherry, established in 2010, stands as a premier institution for technical education and research. Located in Karaikal, the institute has been at the forefront of technological innovation and academic excellence.

Our vision aligns perfectly with Gyanith's theme of human-machine collaboration, as we strive to nurture engineers who can bridge the gap between human creativity and technological advancement.

Our Mission

  • Foster innovation and research excellence
  • Develop industry-ready professionals
  • Promote sustainable technological solutions
  • Encourage entrepreneurship and leadership

Our Vision for the Future

"At Gyanith 25, we envision a future where human ingenuity and machine intelligence work in harmony to create solutions that benefit humanity. Join us in shaping this future together."
